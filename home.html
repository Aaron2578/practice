It is a three-year programme and has been specifically designed for aspirants looking for a career in computers. The course covers all aspects of computers right from the basic fundamentals of computers to database systems & advanced courses like C++, Java etc.To be eligible for this program, you must have completed 10+2 with the minimum 45 per cent to 60 per cent marks. This requirement may vary from college to college. Admission to BSc Computer Science takes place either based on merit or through entrance exam scores. For merit-based admission, the colleges consider the past academic records or the scores of the Class 12 Board exam. On the other hand, some other colleges conduct their admission based on the scores obtained in entrance exams.
